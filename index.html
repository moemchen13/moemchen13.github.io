About me

I began my studies in Tübingen after working as a paramedic in a stressful environment. There, I realized that medicine is often seen as a complete solution, even though most patients never fully recover—an outlook that overlooks long-term quality of life. This realization led me to Bioinformatics, where prevention and well-being are central. In this context, machine learning has become a powerful tool in medicine, and combined with my affinity for mathematics, it is the area where I want to make a difference.
